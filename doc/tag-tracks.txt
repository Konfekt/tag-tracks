==============================================================================

INTRO                                                         *tag-tracks-intro*

tag-tracks provides a HUD for |:tags| for a particular window, making it
easier to trace your steps through a code-base.

==============================================================================

USAGE                                                         *tag-tracks-usage*

Install it with your favorite plugin manager and then use |:TagTracks| to go!

*:TagTracks*    Toggle tag-tracking on and off. Opens a split window with the
              tag stack on display.
